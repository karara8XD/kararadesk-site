Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 11, 2026

1. Who we are

KararaDesk is an independent digital Discord ticket-management service operated by Ahmed Karara (“KararaDesk”, “we”, “us”, or “our”).

2. Information we process

Depending on how a Discord server uses the service, we may process:

  • Discord user IDs, server IDs, channel IDs, role IDs, and usernames;
  • ticket messages, close reasons, internal notes, timestamps, and generated transcripts;
  • subscription status, plan, usage totals, and billing-related identifiers;
  • technical logs needed for reliability, security, and troubleshooting.

3. Why we process information

We process information to create and manage tickets, route support requests, produce transcripts, enforce plan limits, provide subscription access, prevent abuse, and maintain the service.

4. Payment information

Payments are processed by an external merchant-of-record or payment provider. We do not store complete card numbers. We may receive transaction, customer, and subscription identifiers needed to manage access.

5. Data sharing

We share information only when needed to operate the service, comply with law, protect users and the service, or work with infrastructure and payment providers under appropriate terms. We do not sell personal data.

6. Data retention

Information is kept only for as long as reasonably needed for support operations, subscription management, security, legal obligations, and service continuity. Discord server administrators may control or delete certain ticket data using available bot tools.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. No internet service is completely secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

8. Your choices

Users may contact the server administrators responsible for the Discord server where a ticket was created. For requests involving KararaDesk directly, contact us through the official KararaDesk support server.

9. Children

The service is not intended for children below the minimum age required to use Discord or enter into applicable agreements in their jurisdiction.

10. Changes

We may update this policy as the service changes. The effective date above will be revised when material updates are published.

11. Contact

Contact KararaDesk through the official support server.
