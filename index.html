Questions before you start?

No. KararaDesk is a digital software service for Discord communities and support teams.

Can I change plans later?

Yes. Subscription access is designed to be upgraded or changed as your server grows.

What happens when I reach a plan limit?

The bot prevents new activity that exceeds the relevant limit while preserving existing data and tickets.
