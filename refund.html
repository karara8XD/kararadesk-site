Refund Policy

Effective date: July 11, 2026

1. Digital subscription service

KararaDesk is a digital subscription service. Access to paid features may begin immediately after a successful purchase.

2. Refund requests

Refund requests should be submitted within 14 days of the initial purchase. Requests are reviewed individually, including whether the service was materially used, whether access worked as described, and whether duplicate or unauthorized billing occurred.

3. Renewals

Subscription renewals are generally non-refundable once a new billing period begins. Contact us promptly if a renewal was accidental or if you attempted to cancel before renewal and the cancellation did not process correctly.

4. Eligible situations

A full or partial refund may be appropriate when:

  • you were charged more than once for the same subscription period;
  • paid access could not be delivered because of a verified service fault;
  • the transaction was unauthorized and reported promptly;
  • applicable consumer law requires a refund.

5. Non-eligible situations

Refunds may be declined when the service was substantially used, the request is based only on a change of mind after significant use, the account was suspended for abuse, or the request falls outside the applicable period without a legal requirement.

6. How refunds are issued

Approved refunds are returned through the original payment method by the payment provider. Bank and card processing times may vary.

7. Cancellation

You can cancel future renewals without requesting a refund. Cancellation normally keeps the paid plan active until the end of the current billing period.

8. Contact

Submit refund questions through the public support link on the KararaDesk product page. Include the purchase email, transaction reference, date, and reason for the request. Do not send complete card information.
